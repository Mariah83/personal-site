About me

Hello my name is Mariah Palmer

I am a graphic designer living in Niagara Falls. I used to want to be a marketer, but went into graphic design instead, but because of my prior interest I tend to focus on making ads for print and social media, product design and logo design. I am also interested in animation so I enjoy working on motion graphics and learning more about how to make different projects with after effects.

In my spare time I like to keep busy. I volunteer for RGD, and Big brothers and sisters of Canada, run a small online business making fan merchandise and I plan to expand in the future. I have work three jobs pretty constatly and I have been able to make social media posts and graphics for my job at Big B Comics.

I am always trying to learn how to use new equipement and software. I have been leaning sublimation printing, vinyl cutting, embroidery, various other crafts, as well as many different types of software like after affects, adobe premiere, blender and 3D printing software. I am a hard worker and always looking to leanr new things.
